About Us

Home Care Agency with the best care

At AWADAD MUTUAL INC, we believe healthcare should go beyond treatment – it should bring comfort, dignity, and peace of mind. Established with a vision to redefine modern hospital and community care, we focus on delivering both medical expertise and personal support.

Our team of dedicated professionals is passionate about improving the lives of individuals and families through accessible, reliable, and compassionate services. From hospital-based care to home assistance, we combine advanced medical knowledge with a human touch.

With a strong commitment to excellence, integrity, and empathy, we aim to be more than just a hospital – we are your trusted partner in health and wellness.

This Clinic was licensed with the Corporate Affairs Commission Nigeria in the year 2022

Our Vision
To be a leading healthcare provider that transforms lives through compassionate care, innovation, and a deep commitment to community well-being.
Our Mission
To deliver high-quality, patient-centered healthcare that promotes dignity, independence, and overall wellness.

To provide accessible medical, personal, and community support services that meet the diverse needs of individuals and families.

To empower patients and caregivers with the knowledge, care, and resources they need to live healthier, fuller lives.
Our Values
Compassion – We care with empathy, kindness, and respect.
Excellence – We strive for the highest standards in healthcare and support services.
Integrity – We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability in everything we do.
Community – We are dedicated to building healthier, stronger communities through outreach and support.
Innovation – We embrace modern solutions to improve care and enhance patient experiences.
Dignity – We treat every individual with respect, ensuring their comfort and independence.
